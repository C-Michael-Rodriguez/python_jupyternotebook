In November of 2023, the legendary rock band The Beatles released the No. 1 hit song "Now and Then" and topped the Billboard charts in the U.K. The world experienced Beatlemania again for the first time in over a half-century. And if that wasn't enough awesomeness, they followed that up in February of 2025 by winning the Grammy Award for Best Rock Performance with it as the first song assisted by Artificial Intelligence technology to win a Grammy in history. Still continuing to blaze trails as rock poineers up until their very last song recorded with all four of The Beatles; John, Paul, George, & Ringo. AND it was produced by Giles Martin, talented son of the genius Sir George Martin (R.I.P.) Mind blown.

How many bands can say they've done that?!? First of all; NONE. Secondly, especially not with half of the members on the track having passed away by the time it was released (R.I.P. John & George!)
