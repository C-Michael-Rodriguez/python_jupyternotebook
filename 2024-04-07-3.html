The legendary rock band The Beatles released the No. 1 hit song "Now and Then" and topped the Billboard charts in the U.K. in November of 2023. The world experienced Beatlemania again for the first time in over a half-century.

How many bands can say they've done that? Especially with half of the members on the track having passed away by the time it was released. Is it possible to quantify their hit-making prowess, how it relates to other artists that have been on the charts, and maybe gain some insight on their hit-making formula using data science?

This notebook details the journey for those answers...
